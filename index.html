About I.M.B

Inspired by the Karaoke Bars of Tokyo, I.M.B was founded in 2022 to introduce the Japanese Karaoke experience to the heart of London's West End.

We host: 6 private rooms. An Open Mic area. A bar and restaurant. Pool tables. Darts.
